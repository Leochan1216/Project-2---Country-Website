Hong Kong's Cultural Heritage

Hong Kong's culture is a unique mix of Eastern and Western traditions, reflected in its cuisine, festivals, and daily life.

Major Cultural Aspects

Festivals

Chinese New Year, Mid-Autumn Festival, Dragon Boat Festival.

Cuisine

Dim Sum, Roast Goose, Wonton Noodles.

Language

Cantonese is the primary language, with English widely spoken.

Arts

Cantonese Opera, Film Industry, Traditional Calligraphy.
