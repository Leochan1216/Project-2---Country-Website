Hong Kong's Historical Background

Hong Kong's history dates back thousands of years, with influences from Chinese dynasties, colonial rule, and its modern transformation into a global metropolis.

Key Historical Events

1842: Hong Kong became a British colony after the First Opium War.
1941-1945: Japanese occupation during World War II.
1997: The handover of Hong Kong back to China under the "One Country, Two Systems" principle.
